Final Project

Well, this is it! The final project of CSC 106.
So what is it? Easiest way to find out is to click the start button and find out... but here's a hint. It's a throwback to some of the earlier mobile games that were released and plays just like "Line Jumper Type Game" and everyone's favorite, "Nyan Dog" (examples changed for copyright).

To play, hit the start button and then use the "a" and "d" keys to move left and write respectively. Steer your character accross the screen to land on the platforms and move up through the clouds!! (We also gave you two extra lives ^ . o)
